How to play

Placing tiles

Two ways: tap a tile in your rack to select it (it highlights gold), then tap an empty square to drop it — or drag the tile straight onto a square. Tap a placed tile to take it back. Recall returns everything to your rack at once.

Rearranging your rack

Drag a tile left or right within the rack to reorder your letters. Shuffle randomizes them for you.

Challenge

After your opponent plays, Challenge checks their word(s) against the dictionary. If a word isn't valid, their tiles come back and they lose the turn. If every word is valid, the challenge fails and you lose your turn instead — so challenge wisely.

Playing a word

Once your tiles are on the board the Play button shows a live score preview. Press it to confirm your move. The first word must cover the centre star ★. Every word after that must connect to at least one tile already on the board.

Blanks

Tap a blank tile (empty square in your rack) and place it — a letter picker appears. Blanks score 0 points regardless of the letter chosen.

Bingo

Playing all 7 tiles in one turn earns a +50 bonus on top of the word score.

Exchange

Tap Exchange, select the tiles you want to swap, then tap Swap. Only available while at least 7 tiles remain in the bag. Forfeits your turn.

Pass & Shuffle

Pass skips your turn (forfeits it). The game ends if both players pass twice in a row, or after six scoreless turns overall — so the Pass button turns red once you're one round away, and passing then could finish the game. Shuffle randomly reorders your rack without spending a turn.

Turn notifications

Tap the 🔔 in the header to be notified when it becomes your turn while the game is in the background. Tap again to mute. With Web Push set up, notifications arrive even if the browser is fully closed. On iPhone, first add Wurdz to your Home Screen (Share → Add to Home Screen) and open it from there — Apple only allows notifications for installed web apps.

Game end

The game ends when a player uses their last tile with an empty bag, or when both players pass twice in a row (four passes), or after six scoreless turns overall — whichever comes first. Unplayed tiles are deducted from each player's score and added to the player who went out.

Leaving & resigning

Leave just exits to your games list — the game stays put and you can return any time. Resign gives up the game for good: your opponent wins and it's removed from your list. Once a game is finished you can also tap the × on it in your games list to clear it.
