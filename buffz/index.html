    HOW TO PLAY

    Filter the pool of well-known movies and TV shows with the type, decade and genre dropdowns, pick a mode and a difficulty, then race — solo or against up to 5 players. Everyone gets the same questions.

    MIXED — every round is a different kind of question. The party mode.

    PLOTLINES — a tagline or one-line plot is shown; pick the title.

    CASTING — directors, creators and stars: who made it, who's in it, and which title they belong to.

    DETAILS — years, runtimes, genres, studios, countries, original titles, seasons, ratings and box office.

    TIMELINE — drag the titles into release order, oldest at the top, then confirm to reveal the years.

    RANKED — drag the titles into TMDb rating order, lowest at the top.

    Difficulty scales both the number of questions (5 / 10 / 15 / 25) and how many options or titles you juggle at once (3 / 4 / 6 / 8).

    In multiplayer, when everyone's done, tap a player's card at the top to see their answers.

    This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.
