    HOW TO PLAY

    Everyone draws a hand of tiles from a shared bunch and races to arrange all of them into their own connected crossword — every across and down run must be a real word.

    Place tiles by dragging them from your hand onto the grid. Drag a placed tile to move it, or back to your hand to pick it up. Drag empty space to pan the grid; ⌖ re-centres.

    PEEL! — the moment your grid is valid and your hand is empty, hit Peel: every player draws one more tile from the bunch. The game keeps flowing until the bunch runs low.

    DUMP a tile you don't want back into the bunch and draw three in its place (a small penalty). Needs at least three tiles left in the bunch.

    BANANAS! — when the bunch is too low to peel and you finish a valid grid first, you win.
