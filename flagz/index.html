    HOW TO PLAY

    Pick a region, a mode and a difficulty, then race — solo or against up to 5 players. Everyone gets the same questions.

    Difficulty scales the challenge to fit each mode: how many flags or name options you juggle at once (Spotter, Line-up, and the sorting modes), or — in Namedrop, where there's nothing to juggle — the number of questions (EASY 5 · MEDIUM 10 · HARD 15 · ALL the region).

    SPOTTER — a country name is shown; tap its flag.

    LINE-UP — a flag is shown; pick its country from the list.

    NAMEDROP — a flag is shown; type the country (spelling is forgiving).

    A TO Z — drag the flags into alphabetical order without seeing their names, then confirm to reveal.

    HEADCOUNT — drag the flags into population order, smallest at the top.

    LANDMASS — drag the flags into area order, smallest at the top.

    In multiplayer, when everyone's done, tap a player's card at the top to see their answers.
