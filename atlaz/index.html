    HOW TO PLAY

    Pick a region, pick a mode, and race — solo or against up to 5 players. Everyone gets the same questions in the same order.

    PINPOINT — a name is shown; tap where it is, then hit CONFIRM. Zoom with pinch, double-tap or the +/− buttons for the fiddly little ones.

    LINE-UP — one territory lights up; pick its name from the remaining list.

    NAMEDROP — one territory lights up; type its name (spelling is forgiving — accents and punctuation don't matter).

    JIGSAW — the region starts borderless. Drag each piece roughly into place; close enough counts.

    SWEEP — type every name you can remember, fastest full map wins. GIVE UP ends your run; quitters rank below finishers.

    In multiplayer, when everyone's done, tap a player's card at the top to see their map — green right, red wrong.
