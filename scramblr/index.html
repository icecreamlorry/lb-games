    HOW TO PLAY

    Find as many words as you can in the 4×4 grid before the 3-minute timer runs out.

    Make a word two ways: tap letters one by one (each must touch the last — including diagonally) then hit ACCEPT, or drag your finger across the letters and release.

    Words must be 3+ letters, real dictionary words, and can't reuse a tile within the same word.

    Scoring: 3–4 letters = 1, 5 = 2, 6 = 3, 7 = 5, 8+ = 11. A word found by more than one player scores for nobody — so the rarer your words, the better.

    The host starts the round once everyone's in; a 3-2-1 countdown runs for all players, then go!
