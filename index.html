// HOW TO PLAY //
OBJECTIVE
Clear colour groups to score points before the 2-minute timer runs out. Bigger groups score exponentially more.
CONTROLS
TAP / CLICK Flip a tile — reveals its hidden face colour
HOLD 0.5s Activate a group — hold any tile to check its connected same-colour region
CLEARING
A connected group of 4+ tiles showing the same colour clears on a long press. New tiles fall from above to fill the gaps.
SCORING
4 tiles 100 pts
8 tiles 400 pts
12 tiles 900 pts  —  formula: n² × 6.25
BUTTONS
[ FLIP ALL ] Flip every tile to the same face at once
[ RESET ] Abandon the run and return to start
