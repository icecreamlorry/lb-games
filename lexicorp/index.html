FINAL TALLY
    HOW TO PLAY

    Take turns building words from your hand of 7 cards plus 3 shared pool cards. Each word must be 3+ letters and use at least one card from your hand.

    Earn money and stock by word length: longer words pay more, and 6+ letter words also pay stock. Any word with a Q pays double money and stock.

    Patent a letter from your word (once per turn) by paying its cost. After that, every time an opponent uses that letter you collect $1 per letter from the bank.

    Seven patents carry special powers: B J K Q V X Z. Own them for extra doubles, a second word, reusing a card, a free S, or a discard-and-draw.

    When a player's total patent value reaches the threshold, the round finishes and the game ends. Highest total of money + stock + patent value wins (ties go to patent value).
