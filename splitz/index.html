HOW TO PLAY

Everyone takes a hand of tiles from a shared pool and races to arrange all of them into their own connected crossword — every across and down run must be a real word.

Place tiles by dragging them from your hand onto the grid. Drag a placed tile to move it, or back to your hand to pick it up. Drag empty space to pan the grid; ⌖ re-centres.

DRAW! — the moment your grid is valid and your hand is empty, hit Draw: every player takes one more tile from the pool. The game keeps flowing until the pool runs low.

SWAP a tile you don't want for three new ones from the pool (a small penalty). Needs at least three tiles left in the pool.

SPLITZ! — when the pool is too low to draw and you finish a valid grid first, call Splitz to win.
